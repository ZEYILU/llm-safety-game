Can You Spot the Difference?

This interactive demo explores interactional safety — how AI models respond when users describe problematic behaviors, rather than request harmful content.

We tested 4 leading AI models (GPT-5, Claude Sonnet 4.5, Llama 3.3 70B, Qwen 3 Next 80B) across scenarios involving Dark Triad personality traits: Machiavellianism, Narcissism, and Psychopathy.

Your task: Read each scenario, then match the AI responses to their respective models. Can you identify which models challenge the behavior (CORRECTIVE) versus which validate it (REINFORCING)?
